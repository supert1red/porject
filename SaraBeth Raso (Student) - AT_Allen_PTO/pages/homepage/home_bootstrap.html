Our Story 

The AT Allen PTO would like to welcome all new students and families that are registering for the school in the fall. For those who may not know, the PTO is an organization of parents and teachers of AT Allen  Elemantary School. The PTO's goal is to foster a nurturing and caring enviornment that strives for contiuned parent involvement in helping build a better educational enviornment for our children. We also promote a school community where teachers and administrators can do their best work with the resources we can provide and help make school fun. Through fundraising efforts, the PTO is able to donate funds to purchase things like new books for each classroom library, playground equipment, school signage and many other items as well as our time to help our school be the best it can be. For the PTO to be effective and trult representative of the school, it is essential to have parentl involvement from as many parents as possible. There are endless possibilities in ways you can help. Whatever your own level of flexibility, AT Allen Elementary PTO and school staff appreciates parent volunteers.
